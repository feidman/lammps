    https://docs.lammps.org/units.html

    https://docs.lammps.org/Commands_compute.html

    https://docs.lammps.org/compute.html

    https://docs.lammps.org/pair_eam.html

(5) Use Google search bars on the LAMMPS website

    many hits come up, the first one is to the pair_style eam doc page

(b) Type "metallic glass Mg Zn" in the search bar on the LAMMPS home page
    3 hits come up, 2 of them to paper abstracts on the LAMMPS website
